Withdrawal Policy

Introduction

This Withdrawal Policy explains the rules and procedures for withdrawing funds from your account. Please read this policy carefully.

Eligibility for Withdrawal

Withdrawals can be requested if the following conditions are met:

  • Your account is verified with valid identification documents.
  • The withdrawal amount meets the minimum threshold set by us.

Processing Time

Withdrawal requests are processed within 3-5 business days. However, delays may occur due to:

  • Verification of documents.
  • Bank processing times.
  • National holidays or unforeseen events.

Fees and Charges

Depending on the withdrawal method chosen, certain fees may apply. These will be disclosed at the time of withdrawal.

Payment Methods

Funds can be withdrawn through the following methods:

  • Bank Transfer
  • Payment Gateways (e.g., PayPal, Stripe)
  • Digital Wallets

Ensure the details provided are accurate to avoid delays.

Restrictions

Withdrawals may be restricted or denied in the following cases:

  • Suspicious or fraudulent activity.
  • Violation of our Terms and Conditions.
  • Insufficient funds in your account.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance with withdrawals, contact our support team at support@himstar.com.
